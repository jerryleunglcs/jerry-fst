PROGRAMMES

Associate of engineering

Training students to apply mathematical methods and analytical skills, and engineering methods and tools, to basic engineering design problems and derive solutions. Also, the Associate of Engineering helps students who aim for producing
engineering solutions according to system specifications and standards.

Associate of Science

In this programme, our professionals will introduce the concepts and methods fundamental to basic science. So our students are able to interpret measurement data using quantitative methods, perform skilled tasks responsibly, in accordance with ethical norms, and produce outputs that meet quality standards after taking the courses.

Associate of Science in

Airport Operations & Aviation Logistics

Our students may able to develop a broad theoretical understanding in airport operations, airline ground operations, and air freight logistics operations. They also can work competently at associate level in professional jobs in aviation and logistics industry and to pursue further studies at degree level.

Associate of Science in

Aviation & Pilot Studies

Through this programme, our students are able to handle basic flight training skills for piloting and the skills for aviation operations.

Associate of Science in

Business Analysis

The Associate of Science in Business Analyze is suitable for the students who aim for mathematical and statistical skills to formulate and solve business problems. Through this course, they can use a range of statistical and information technology applications to solve business problems. They may also apply business sense and ethical principles in the undertaking of statistical work.

Associate of Science in

Creative & Interactive Media Production

This programme aim to train student to relate creativity, aesthetics and media technology to areas of media design and production. Students can also apply media and information methodologies, both independently and within a team, to develop interactive multimedia systems and enhance the levels of quality in production process.

Associate of Science in

Environmental Studies

Upon successful completion of this programme, students will be able to explain concepts and methods fundamental to environmental studies. They also have the ability to analyze and interpret data using quantitative methods, and information technology systems, particularly with regard to environmental data, and especially for the purpose of planning and management.

Associate of Science in

Information Sysytems Development

Students will be able to use a range of tools, programming languages, methodologies, and quantitative skills to undertake information systems development work. By studying in the Associate of Science in Information Systems Development, students may apply technical knowledge and skills, and well-proven software models and work responsibly as a team to develop secure, robust, and reliable quality business information systems.

Associate of Science in

Network & Systems Administration

This programme target on teaching students to use a range of tools, programming languages, methodologies, and quantitative skills to undertake information systems development work. By studying in the Associate of Science in Network and System Administration, students may apply technical knowledge and skills, and work responsibly in the design, installation, and configuration of secure computer systems and networks.
